Jammu & Kashmir - Paradise on Earth

The Enchanting Culture of Jammu & Kashmir

Experience the serene and culturally rich atmosphere of Jammu & Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth." The region's culture is a beautiful blend of various traditions, reflected in its music, dance forms like Rouf and Bachha Nagma, and exquisite handicrafts such as Pashmina and Kashmiri carpets. The warmth and hospitality of the Kashmiri people are truly heartwarming.

A Taste of Kashmiri Cuisine

Kashmiri cuisine is renowned for its aromatic spices and unique flavors. Indulge in the famous Wazwan, a multi-course meal featuring dishes like Rogan Josh, Yakhni, and Gushtaba. Savor the traditional Kahwa, a fragrant green tea infused with saffron, cardamom, and almonds.



Key Attractions in Jammu & Kashmir

  • Srinagar: Explore the summer capital, known for its stunning Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens like Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh, and the Shankaracharya Temple offering panoramic views.


    Travel Rate: ₹15,000 per person (4 days)
    Food Provided: Breakfast and Dinner (Kashmiri Cuisine)
    Mode of Transport: Private AC Car & Shikara Ride







  • Gulmarg: Visit the "Meadow of Flowers," famous for its breathtaking landscapes and skiing opportunities during winter. Enjoy the scenic gondola ride for mesmerizing views.


    Travel Rate: ₹18,000 per person (3 days)
    Food Provided: Breakfast
    Mode of Transport: Private AC Car

  • Pahalgam: Explore the "Valley of Shepherds," known for its lush green meadows, pristine rivers, and trekking trails. Visit Betaab Valley and Aru Valley for stunning natural beauty.


    Travel Rate: ₹16,000 per person (3 days)
    Food Provided: Breakfast
    Mode of Transport: Private AC Car
