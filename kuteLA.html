Ladakh - The Land of High Passes

Unique Culture and Heritage of Ladakh

Discover the stark beauty and rich cultural heritage of Ladakh, often called "Little Tibet." The culture is deeply influenced by Tibetan Buddhism, evident in its monasteries (gompas), prayer wheels, and vibrant festivals. Experience the traditional Ladakhi music and the warmth of its people.

Flavors of Ladakh

Ladakhi cuisine is hearty and adapted to the cold desert climate. Try Thukpa (noodle soup), Momos (dumplings), and Skyu (a traditional stew). Don't miss the butter tea, a staple drink in the region.
